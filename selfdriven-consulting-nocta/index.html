Process

How an engagement works

Six steps from brief to outcome — most traditional firms stop at step 4 and call it done.

01
Brief & Scope
You describe the challenge. Our intake agent structures it against the 8 Areas of Focus and surfaces a scoping proposal within 24 hours.
→
02
Conductor Assignment
A human conductor is assigned as accountable lead. Their KERI AID + ACDC credential is shared with you — full transparency.
→
03
Agent Deployment
Domain-specific AI agents are delegated via KERI dip events. Each agent has scoped, time-limited authority for your engagement only.
→
04
Living Deliverables
Outputs are structured documents, not static decks. They update as context shifts. Versioned. KERI-anchored. Auditable.
→
05
Review & Iterate
Conductor-led review sessions. Findings fed back into agents. Every iteration is logged on the KEL — no lost context.
→
06
Embed & Exit
We don't create dependency. All frameworks, models, and credentials export to your systems. You own everything we build.
